A `PieceStore` is an object that can store and retrieve pieces
from some local storage. The `PieceStore` additionally keeps
an index of pieces.

`数据片存储`是一个可以从一些本地存储中进行存储和检索片段的对象。`片存储`还保存了块的索引。
